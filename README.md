This reposity describes the materials and methods used to build an underwater camera equipped with water quality sensors (temperature and dissolved oxygen) using Raspberry Pi and Arduino. The set ups described below were tested and deployed in the Old Ausable Channel (OAC) in Lambton Shores, Ontario ğŸ‡¨ğŸ‡¦. The OAC is a narrow, low flow freshwater stream near the south shore of Lake Huron. Due to its natural history the OAC contains a high level of freshwater fish diversity and is home to multiple fish species-at-risk. 

The endangered Lake Chubsucker (*Erimyzon sucetta*) calls the OAC home and we were able to use these cameras to capture them across the channel. As seen here in this example still of two adult Lake Chubsucker:
